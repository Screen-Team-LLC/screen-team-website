Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Overview

The Screen Team LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates screenteamllc.com. This page explains what information we collect through this website and how we use it. We keep it simple because our operation is simple.

Information We Collect

When you submit the contact form on this site, you provide:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number (optional)
  • Your email address (via the form submission service)
  • A description of the work you need done

We do not collect payment information, account credentials, or any sensitive personal data through this website. We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising.

How We Use Your Information

The only purpose for collecting your contact information is to respond to your quote request or inquiry. Chris Westcott will use it to get back to you about your job - nothing else.

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. Period.

Form Submission Service

Contact forms on this site are processed by Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party form handling service. When you submit a form, your data passes through their servers and is delivered to us by email. Formspree's own privacy policy governs how they handle form data in transit. We do not store your data on Formspree's platform beyond the email delivery.

Data Retention

Your contact information is retained only as long as needed to complete your job inquiry or transaction. We don't maintain a customer database or mailing list.

Your Rights

If you'd like us to delete any information you've submitted, or if you have any questions about how your data is handled, contact Chris directly:

  • Phone/Text: (727) 386-6562

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we'll update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. This site doesn't collect data for marketing, so major changes are unlikely.
