Lanai Screen Repair & Replacement

A lanai is one of the best parts of a Florida home — outdoor living space that's comfortable even in peak bug season. When the screening tears, sags, or pulls away from the frame, that space becomes a lot less enjoyable.

The Screen Team specializes in lanai screen repairs and full rescreens. Whether you've got one torn panel or an entire enclosure that needs a refresh, Chris handles the job with clean workmanship and no unnecessary upsell.

What We Cover

  • Individual lanai panel replacements
  • Full lanai rescreens from frame to frame
  • Screen door repairs, alignments, and replacements
  • Spline replacement for panels pulling away from the frame
  • Pet-resistant and heavy-duty mesh options
  • No-see-um fine mesh for areas with smaller insects

Common Lanai Issues We Fix

  • Torn or punctured screen panels
  • Sagging mesh that won't stay taut
  • Screen pulling out of the frame channel
  • Oxidized or discolored mesh blocking the view
  • Doors that don't seal or latch properly
