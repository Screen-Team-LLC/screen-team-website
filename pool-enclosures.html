Pool Cage Rescreens & Repairs

Pool enclosures in Florida take constant abuse - UV exposure, heavy rain, wind, and wildlife all wear down screen material. When your pool cage screens start sagging, tearing, or letting bugs through, a rescreen restores the function and appearance without replacing the entire structure.

The Screen Team handles pool cage rescreens from individual panel replacements to full enclosure jobs. If the aluminum frame is sound, a rescreen is the smart call over a full rebuild.

What We Handle

  • Full pool cage rescreens - top to bottom
  • Single panel and section replacements
  • Screen door repairs and replacements
  • Re-splinning loose or pulled-away panels
  • Heavy-duty and pet-resistant screen upgrades
  • No-see-um (fine mesh) screen installation

Benefits of a Fresh Rescreen

  • Keeps mosquitoes, no-see-ums, and debris out of the pool area
  • Reduces leaf and organic material in the water
  • Restores the clean, maintained look of your enclosure
  • Protects your investment before bigger structural repairs are needed
  • Improves airflow versus sagging, clogged screens

Pool Enclosure FAQs

Answers about pool cage rescreening in Tampa Bay.

In Florida's sun, humidity, and storm conditions, pool cage screening typically lasts 5-10 years before it starts degrading noticeably. Signs it's time: visible tears, sagging, loose spline, or mesh pulling away from the frame.

Fiberglass is the standard for most pool cages - it's flexible, rust-resistant, and holds up well in Florida's climate. If you have pets or want extra durability, aluminum or pet-screen options are available. Chris will recommend the right grade for your situation.

Yes - we'll replace only the sections that need it. You don't have to do the whole cage if the rest of the screening is still in serviceable condition. We won't push a full rescreen if a targeted approach is the right call.

Standard fiberglass screening keeps mosquitoes out effectively. For no-see-ums (tiny biting midges), a finer mesh called "no-see-um screen" is available as an upgrade. Ask Chris about this option when you get your quote.
