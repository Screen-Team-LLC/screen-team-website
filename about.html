The Screen Team LLC is a Tampa Bay screen repair and enclosure business owned and operated by Chris Westcott. When you call, you're talking to the person doing the work — not a call center, not a dispatcher routing you to a subcontractor chain.

Chris has built the business on a simple model: show up, do the job right, be honest about what it costs, and stand behind the work when the job is done. That's it.

The focus is residential screen repair — pool cage rescreens, lanai screening, window screens, garage screens, and gutter work — throughout Pinellas and Hillsborough County communities in the greater Tampa Bay area.

How it works

Simple process, start to finish.

No complicated steps. You call, Chris comes out, the job gets done.

1. Call or text

Reach Chris directly at (727) 386-6562, or fill out the contact form. Describe the job — type of screen, approximate size, what's wrong. That's all he needs to get started.

2. Get a quote

Chris will give you a straight, honest estimate — over the phone for standard jobs, or in person for larger or more complex enclosures. No inflated numbers, no sales tactics.

Chris handles the work himself from start to finish. Clean materials, proper installation, nothing left half-done. Payment is collected on completion, after you've seen the work.
