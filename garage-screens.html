Garage Screen Installation & Repair

Florida garages don't have to be hot, stuffy bug traps. A properly installed garage screen system lets you work in the garage with the door up — airflow in, pests and debris out — without sacrificing security or convenience.

The Screen Team handles garage screen installations and repairs. Whether you need a simple mesh panel for an existing door frame or a repair on a system that's already in place, Chris will size up the job and give you an honest assessment of what makes sense.

What We Handle

  • Garage screen panel installation for single and double garage bays
  • Repair or replacement of torn or damaged garage screen mesh
  • Retractable garage screen repairs and panel replacements
  • Magnetic closure and zipper entry screen systems
  • Heavy-duty mesh options for high-traffic or commercial use

Why a Garage Screen Matters in Florida

  • Keeps mosquitoes, palmetto bugs, and no-see-ums out while the door is up
  • Reduces heat buildup by allowing cross-ventilation
  • Keeps leaves, debris, and animals out of the garage space
  • Makes the garage usable as a workshop or hangout area year-round

Garage Screen FAQs

Answers about garage screen installation and repair in Tampa Bay.

We work with panel-style garage screens (framed panels installed in the door opening) and retractable garage screen systems. These let you open the garage to airflow while keeping bugs and debris out. Chris will show you options that fit your garage setup.

Most standard single and double-car garage openings can accommodate a screen system. Custom or oversized openings may require specific solutions. Chris will measure and advise when he comes out for an estimate.

Yes — a properly fitted garage screen is an effective barrier against mosquitoes, flies, and most insects. For very fine insects like no-see-ums, a finer mesh option is available. Proper installation around the perimeter makes the biggest difference.

Small tears or damaged panels in a framed system can often be repaired or individually replaced without replacing the entire system. Retractable systems may need component replacement depending on the damage. Chris will assess and give you the honest answer.
